# The Knowledge Garden

A short story exploring how ideas grow and connect, told through the metaphor of a garden.

## Synopsis

A librarian discovers that her notes have begun to grow like plants, forming connections she never intended. As the knowledge garden expands, she must decide whether to let it grow wild or impose structure.
