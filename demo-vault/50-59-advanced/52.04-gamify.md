# Gamified Tasks

Turn your productivity into a game with XP, levels, streaks, and rewards.

## Enabling

Enable **Gamify** in Settings > Features. It appears as a left sidebar panel.

## Features

### XP & Levels

- Earn XP for completing tasks, writing notes, and daily streaks
- Level up as you accumulate XP
- View your current level and progress bar

### Daily Streaks

- Maintain a streak by completing at least one task per day
- Streak counter shows consecutive active days
- Bonus XP multiplier for long streaks

### Rewards

- Spend earned coins on custom rewards you define
- Track reward purchases in the Rewards tab

### Quick Summary

Use the command `Quest: XP Summary` to see your current stats:

> Level, total XP, today's XP, and current streak.

> [!tip] Command Palette
> Press `Cmd+P` and type "Quest" to quickly access XP summary and the quest panel.
