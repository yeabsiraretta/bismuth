# Zettelkasten Method

The **Zettelkasten** ("slip box") method is a knowledge management system based on atomic, interlinked notes.

## Core Principles

1. **Atomicity** — Each note captures exactly one idea
2. **Linking** — Notes connect to related concepts via [[wikilink|wikilinks]]
3. **Emergent Structure** — Organization emerges from connections, not folders
4. **Your Own Words** — Rewrite ideas to deepen understanding

## Zettelkasten vs Johnny.Decimal

| Aspect | Zettelkasten | [[11.02-johnny-decimal-system|Johnny.Decimal]] |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Structure | Flat, linked | Hierarchical, numbered |
| Discovery | Follow links | Browse folders |
| Scale | Infinite | Max 10 areas |

In Bismuth, these complement each other — Johnny.Decimal organizes the **filesystem**, while Zettelkasten organizes **ideas**.
