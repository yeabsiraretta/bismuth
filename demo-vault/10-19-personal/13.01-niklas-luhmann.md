# Niklas Luhmann

German sociologist and one of the most important social theorists of the 20th century. Creator of the original [[11.03-zettelkasten-method|Zettelkasten]] system.

## Key Facts

- Maintained a physical Zettelkasten with over **90,000** index cards
- Published more than **70 books** and nearly **400 scholarly articles**
- His slip-box system is the inspiration behind modern PKM tools like [[11.01-welcome-to-bismuth|Bismuth]]

> [!quote] Luhmann on his Zettelkasten
> "I don't think everything on my own. Most of it happens in the slip-box."
